**Problem:** New users were dropping off during onboarding, before they ever reached the parts of the app that delivered value.

**Approach:** Combined qualitative research with hands-on Figma design and a full Flutter/Dart rebuild of the onboarding and subscription flow — research through to shipped production code.

**Outcome:** A redesigned onboarding flow that gets users to value faster, with the subscription step reworked alongside it to reduce friction at the point users were most likely to leave.
