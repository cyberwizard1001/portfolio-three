**Problem:** Mental health practitioners lose 15–20 minutes of every session to admin — notes, summaries, follow-ups — time that could go to the client instead.

**Approach:** An MSc dissertation exploring what a tool designed specifically for counsellors could look like, with particular focus on the parasocial dynamics and trust implications of bringing AI into a therapeutic context.

**Outcome:** A research-led concept for a counselling companion tool, grounded in how practitioners actually work and what they'd need to trust an AI assistant with session admin.
