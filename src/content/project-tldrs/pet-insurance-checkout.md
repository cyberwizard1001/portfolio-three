**Problem:** The review screen was the last step before payment — and the point where most users were dropping off.

**Approach:** Ran 11 internal interviews and combined the findings with Hotjar session recordings and usability heuristics to pinpoint where trust and clarity broke down on the review screen.

**Constraints:** Any change had to stay within FCA regulatory requirements for insurance products — pricing, disclosures, and consent couldn't be simplified away.

**Outcome:** Redesigned the review screen (built in MudBlazor) to surface the information users actually needed at that step, reducing confusion at the final hurdle before conversion.
